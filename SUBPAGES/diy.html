DIY beauty masks

Treat yourself at home with one of our DIY face masks. They all use three ingredients that you'll most likely find in your own kitchen.

1 Glowing skin

Ingredients:

  • 1tbsp raw honey
  • 1tbsp turmeric powder
  • 1tbsp sea salt

    • How to:

    Mix all the ingredients well, apply to face. Leave on for 15-20 minutes then rinse off with lukewarm water.

    Results:

    The sea salt gently exfoliates the skin and fights with clogged pores,the honey have great bacteria fighting properties. And the non-plus-ultra is the turmeric. It helps to reduce inflammation and gives a healthy glow to your skin.

    2 Full of antioxidants

    Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 tbsp green tea powder

    • How to:

    Mash the avocado and combine with the green tea powder until you get a smooth mixture. Apply to face, leave on for 15-20 minutes then rinse off with lukewarm water.

    Results:

    The avocado contains a bunch of nice hydrating elements and the green tea fill your skin with energy. This easy mixture give a nourishing kick to your skin, and improve the overall skin health.

    3 Oatmeal for balancing

    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1/2 cup oatmeal powder
  • 5drops of lemon juice
  • 1 pinch of cinnamon powder
  • 1 tbsp honey

    • How to:

    Mix all the ingredients well,let it sit for 10 minutes. Apply to face, leave on for 15-20 minutes then rinse off with lukewarm water.

    Results:

    The lemon and oatmeal balance the skin PH, cinnamon reduce inflammation. The whole mixture also remove acne scars. Use it on a weekly basis.
